About

MARLON
PEÑA

Born in the Dominican Republic and raised in Miami, Marlon P discovered his passion for visual storytelling at an early age, earning a degree in Film and the Arts before building a career defined by innovation, discipline, and a singular creative vision.

With more than two decades in the industry and 20B+ combined views across his catalog, he has directed some of the most culturally defining music videos of his generation, including Daddy Yankee and Snow's Con Calma, the most-watched video on YouTube in 2019.

His work spans music videos, commercial campaigns, long-form content, and national television, with credits on Good Morning America, The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, Jimmy Kimmel Live, and La Voz. Now stepping fully into narrative filmmaking, Marlon recently served as Executive Producer on a feature film, with multiple narrative film projects in development.
